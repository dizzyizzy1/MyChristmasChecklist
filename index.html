Getting ready for Christmas

What to get for your home:

A Christmas Wreath is a classic staple for any home, and you can either make your own or buy one. You can buy this wreath at Target. A great choice to decorate the front of your home.
And if you need a Christmas tree, there are alot of wonderful Christmas Tree farms in Florida for you to choose from, and not only will it be a beautiful addition to your home, it can be a fun family activity to get into the holiday spirit.

Top places to visit for Christmas

One of the most iconic places to visit in Florida for Christmas is Santas Enchanted Forest. Its a staple for kids and adults alike, where you can go on rides, see shows and even take a picture with Santa Claus himself.

Another wonderful place to visit in FLorida for Christmas is of course, Disney World. And yes its a given to check out the rides, and meet all your favorite chracters, but it is also worth checking out the the Candlelight Processionals at Epcot. Each night, a celebrity narrator tells the story of Christmas, It recounts the biblical tale of Jesus being born in Bethlehem, sung by a choir mixed with local high-school students and Disneys very own choir.
